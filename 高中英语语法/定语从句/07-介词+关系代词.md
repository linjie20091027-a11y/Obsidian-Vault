# 介词 + 关系代词

## 一、基本结构

```
介词 + which（指物）
介词 + whom（指人）
```

**that 和 who 不能直接用于介词后！**

> ✅ This is the room **in which** I lived.
> ❌ This is the room **in that** I lived.

> ✅ The man **with whom** I talked is a doctor.
> ❌ The man **with who** I talked is a doctor.

---

## 二、介词的确定方法

### 1. 根据定语从句中动词的搭配

> This is the book **for which** I paid 50 yuan.
> （pay for the book → for which）

> The girl **to whom** I spoke is from London.
> （speak to the girl → to whom）

> Is this the key **for which** you are looking?
> （look for the key → for which）
> **注意**：look for 这类动词+介词固定短语，介词通常不前置，而说 `the key (that) you are looking for`

### 2. 根据定语从句中形容词的搭配

> The subject **about which** he is excited is physics.
> （be excited about → about which）

> The man **with whom** she is familiar is her boss.
> （be familiar with → with whom）

### 3. 根据先行词与介词的搭配

> This is the farm **on which** my father worked.
> （on the farm → on which）

> The speed **at which** the car runs is 100 km/h.
> （at the speed → at which）

> The day **on which** I met her was sunny.
> （on the day → on which = when）

### 4. 根据句子意思

> This is the man **without whom** we can't finish the job.
> （没有他我们完不成工作 → without whom）

> Water, **without which** man cannot live, is very important.
> （没有水人类无法生存 → without which）

---

## 三、介词 + which 与关系副词互换

这是高考高频考点！

| 介词 + which | 关系副词 |
|-------------|---------|
| at/in/on/during + which | when |
| at/in/on + which | where |
| for + which | why |

### 例句

> The day **on which** (= **when**) I was born was rainy.
> （我出生的那天下着雨。）

> The factory **in which** (= **where**) he works is large.
> （他工作的工厂很大。）

> The reason **for which** (= **why**) he left is unknown.
> （他离开的原因不为人知。）

### 介词的选择要点

| 先行词 | 常用介词 | 例句 |
|-------|---------|------|
| 具体某一天 | on | the day **on which** |
| 年、月 | in | the year **in which** |
| 时刻 | at | the moment **at which** |
| 城市、国家 | in | the city **in which** |
| 车站、学校 | at | the station **at which** |
| 速度、温度 | at | the speed **at which** |
| 价格 | at/for | the price **at/for which** |
| 方式 | in | the way **in which** |

## 四、介词的位置

### 位置 1：介词在关系代词前（正式）

> This is the house **in which** I was born.
> The man **with whom** I went there was my father.

### 位置 2：介词在从句末尾（口语化）

> This is the house **(which) I** was born **in**.
> The man **(whom) I** went there **with** was my father.

### 注意事项

1. **介词后置时，关系代词可以省略**（作宾语）：
   > This is the house I was born in.

2. **固定动词短语的介词一般不前置**：
   > The children **(whom)** she is **looking after** are very young. ✅
   > The children **after whom** she is looking are very young.（极少用，不自然）

   常见的固定短语：look after, look for, look into, take care of, pay attention to 等，介词通常不提前。

3. **介词后置时也用 who/that**：
   > The man **(who/that)** she is waiting **for** is her husband. ✅
   > The man **for whom** she is waiting is her husband. ✅（正式）

## 五、名词/代词 + 介词 + which/whom

这是较高级的语法结构，常在阅读中出现。

> He has three sons, **two of whom** are doctors.
> （他有三个儿子，其中两个是医生。）

> She bought many books, **most of which** were expensive.
> （她买了许多书，大部分很贵。）

> The students, **some of whom** I know, are from China.
> （这些学生，其中有些我认识，来自中国。）

### 常见结构

| 结构 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| 数词 + of + which/whom | He has ten books, three of which are novels. |
| 代词 + of + which/whom | She has five cats, all of which are white. |
| the + 名词 + of + which/whom | I saw a house, the roof of which was red. |
| 最高级 + of + which/whom | He wrote many novels, the best of which is this one. |

### 例句

> The old man has two daughters, **both of whom** are teachers.
> （老人有两个女儿，两个都是老师。）

> There are 40 students in our class, **most of whom** are from the countryside.
> （我们班有40个学生，大部分来自农村。）

> He told me several stories, **none of which** was true.
> （他给我讲了好几个故事，没有一个是真的。）

## 六、the way 作先行词的特殊处理

当先行词是 **the way**（方式）时，定语从句的关系词有三种情况：

> I don't like the way **in which** he speaks.（最正式）
> I don't like the way **that** he speaks.（常用）
> I don't like the way **/** he speaks.（最口语，省略）

也可以说：
> I don't like the way **how** he speaks.（虽然语法书中常见此说法，但标准英语中 the way 和 how 一般不同时出现）

**注意**：标准用法中，the way 后面不用 how。要么用 in which，要么用 that，要么省略。

## 七、介词 + which/whom 的高级用法

### 1. 表示时间段的 during which

> He stayed there for three months, **during which** he learned a lot.
> （他在那里待了三个月，期间学到了很多。）

### 2. 表示根据/依据的 by which

> This is the standard **by which** we judge the quality.
> （这就是我们判断质量的标准。）

### 3. 表示方向/目标的 to which

> This is the problem **to which** we should pay attention.
> = This is the problem **(which)** we should pay attention **to**.
> （这是我们应该注意的问题。）

### 4. 表示范围/整体与部分的 of which/whom

> She mentioned a novel, **the author of which** I don't know.
> （她提到了一本小说，我不知道它的作者是谁。）

## 八、常见错误辨析

### 错误 1：介词后用 that
> ❌ The house **in that** I live is big.
> ✅ The house **in which** I live is big.

### 错误 2：介词后用 who
> ❌ The man **with who** I went is Tom.
> ✅ The man **with whom** I went is Tom.

### 错误 3：介词缺失（该用介词时没用）
> ❌ I don't know the pen he is writing.
> ✅ I don't know the pen **with which** he is writing.
> = I don't know the pen **(that)** he is writing **with**.

### 错误 4：固定短语介词不当提前
> ❌ The children **after whom** she looks are lovely.（不自然）
> ✅ The children **(whom)** she looks **after** are lovely.

## 九、介词 + 关系代词解题三步法

1. **还原法**：将先行词放回从句中，看需要什么介词
   > The book ___ I paid 50 yuan.
   → 还原：I paid 50 yuan **for** the book.
   → 答案：**for which**

2. **搭配法**：看动词/形容词与介词的固定搭配
   > The man ___ she is married.
   → be married **to** sb
   → 答案：**to whom**

3. **习惯法**：看先行词的习惯搭配
   > The speed ___ the car runs.
   → 习惯说 at the speed
   → 答案：**at which**
