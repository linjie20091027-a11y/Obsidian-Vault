# 定语从句常见考点与练习

## 一、高考常见考点汇总

### 考点 1：关系代词 vs 关系副词的判断

**解题关键**：分析从句成分——缺主语/宾语/表语→用关系代词；不缺主宾表、缺状语→用关系副词。

**真题示例**：
> 1. The house ___ I lived ten years ago has been pulled down.
> 答案：**where**（live 不及物，不缺宾语，缺地点状语）

> 2. The house ___ I visited ten years ago has been pulled down.
> 答案：**that/which**（visit 及物，缺宾语）

---

### 考点 2：必须用 that 的情况

记忆口诀：**"代高序，疑问词，人物并，不定与众"**

**真题示例**：
> 3. This is the best film ___ I have ever seen.
> 答案：**that**（先行词前有最高级）

> 4. Who is the man ___ is standing over there?
> 答案：**that**（主句以 who 开头，避免重复）

> 5. They talked about the people and things ___ they remembered.
> 答案：**that**（先行词同时包含人和物）

---

### 考点 3：非限制性定语从句不可用 that

**真题示例**：
> 6. The book, ___ I bought yesterday, is very popular.
> 答案：**which**（非限制性定语从句）

> 7. My father, ___ is a doctor, goes to work early every day.
> 答案：**who**（非限制性定语从句，指人）

---

### 考点 4：介词 + which/whom

**真题示例**：
> 8. This is the car ___ which I paid a lot of money.
> 答案：**for**（pay for -> for which）

> 9. The man ___ whom I shook hands is a famous scientist.
> 答案：**with**（shake hands with -> with whom）

---

### 考点 5：as 引导定语从句

**真题示例**：
> 10. ___ is known to all, the earth moves around the sun.
> 答案：**As**（句首只能用 as）

> 11. He is such a person ___ is always ready to help others.
> 答案：**as**（such ... as，as 在从句中作主语）

---

### 考点 6：抽象地点名词用 where

**真题示例**：
> 12. We are in a situation ___ we must take action at once.
> 答案：**where**（situation 抽象地点）

> 13. He has reached the stage ___ he can make his own decisions.
> 答案：**where**（stage 抽象地点）

---

### 考点 7：whose 表所属关系

**真题示例**：
> 14. The girl ___ mother is a nurse studies in our school.
> 答案：**whose**

> 15. He lives in a room ___ window faces south.
> 答案：**whose**（= the window of which）

---

### 考点 8：the same ... as/that 辨析

**真题示例**：
> 16. This is the same pen ___ I lost yesterday.（同一支笔）
> 答案：**that**

> 17. This is the same pen ___ I bought last week.（同款，非同一支）
> 答案：**as**

---

### 考点 9：such ... as/that 辨析

**真题示例**：
> 18. He is such a clever boy ___ everyone likes.（定语从句）
> 答案：**as**（as 作 likes 的宾语）

> 19. He is such a clever boy ___ everyone likes him.（状语从句）
> 答案：**that**（that 不做成分，从句完整）

---

### 考点 10：one of + 复数名词的主谓一致

**真题示例**：
> 20. He is one of the students who ___ been to Beijing.
> 答案：**have**（先行词是 students，复数）

> 21. He is the only one of the students who ___ been to Beijing.
> 答案：**has**（先行词是 the only one，单数）

---

## 二、综合练习题

### 基础题（填空）

1. The man ___ is talking to my mother is my English teacher.
2. The book ___ I bought yesterday is very interesting.
3. I will never forget the day ___ I joined the army.
4. The reason ___ he was late was that his bike was broken.
5. This is the room ___ my grandfather once lived.
6. The girl to ___ I spoke is from Beijing.
7. He has a son, ___ is a doctor.
8. ___ is known to all, China is a developing country.
9. He talked about the people and things ___ he remembered.
10. Who is the boy ___ is standing by the window?

---

### 提高题（填空）

11. This is the best film ___ has been shown this year.
12. He is the only person ___ was invited to the party.
13. I don't like the way ___ he speaks to his mother.
14. The day ___ I spent with my family was unforgettable.
15. The factory ___ my father works is far from here.
16. The factory ___ my father visited is far from here.
17. He gave me such good advice ___ I will never forget.
18. He gave me such good advice ___ I will never forget it.
19. The room, the windows of ___ face south, is my bedroom.
20. He has reached a point ___ nothing can change his mind.

---

### 改错题（每句有一处错）

21. This is the pen with that I write every day.
22. He is one of the students who has passed the exam.
23. The book, that I bought yesterday, is very good.
24. Which is known to all, smoking is harmful to health.
25. The day which I was born was a sunny day.
26. I don't know the reason that he was late.
27. He is such a good student that we all like.
28. The news that he told was exciting.
29. The news that he won the game was exciting.
30. I will never forget the days when I spent with you.

---

### 翻译题

31. 他是我唯一信任的人。
32. 我们住在一个窗户朝南的房间里。
33. 众所周知，中国是一个有着悠久历史的国家。
34. 这就是我去年工作过的那家工厂。
35. 他有一个哥哥，哥哥是解放军。

## 三、答案与解析

### 基础题答案

1. **who** — 指人，在从句中作主语
2. **that/which** — 指物，在从句中作宾语（可省略）
3. **when** — 先行词 the day，从句缺时间状语
4. **why** — 先行词 the reason，从句缺原因状语
5. **where** — 先行词 the room，从句缺地点状语
6. **whom** — 介词 to 后指人用 whom
7. **who** — 非限制性定语从句，指人
8. **As** — 句首，指代整个主句
9. **that** — 先行词包含人和物
10. **that** — 主句以 who 开头，避免重复

### 提高题答案

11. **that** — 先行词前有最高级 best
12. **that** — 先行词前有 the only
13. **that / in which / 省略** — the way 的定语从句
14. **that/which** — spent 缺宾语（spent the day）
15. **where** — works 是不及物动词，缺地点状语
16. **that/which** — visited 是及物动词，缺宾语
17. **as** — such ... as 定语从句，as 作 forget 的宾语
18. **that** — such ... that 状语从句，从句完整
19. **which** — the windows of which = whose windows
20. **where** — point 是抽象地点，缺地点状语

### 改错题答案

21. **that → which**（介词后不能用 that）
22. **has → have**（one of + 复数名词，先行词是 students）
23. **that → which**（非限制性定语从句不能用 that）
24. **Which → As**（句首不能用 which 指代整句话）
25. **which → when**（born 不缺宾语，缺时间状语）
26. **that → why** 或 **改句意**（从句不缺主宾，缺原因状语；若 reason 作 gave 的宾语则用 that，但这里显然是状语）
27. **that → as**（such ... as 定语从句，as 作 like 的宾语）
28. ✅（这句是对的，that 作 told 的宾语——但缺少宾语时 that 也可省。这句语法正确，但需要看具体想表达的意思）
29. ✅（同位语从句，语法正确）
30. **when → that/which**（spent 缺宾语→ spent the days）

### 翻译题答案

31. He is the only person **that** I trust.
    = He is the only person **whom** I trust.

32. We live in a room **whose** window faces south.
    = We live in a room **the window of which** faces south.

33. **As** is known to all, China is a country with a long history.
    = China, **as** is known to all, is a country with a long history.

34. This is the factory **where** I worked last year.
    = This is the factory **in which** I worked last year.

35. He has a brother, **who** is a PLA man.
    = He has a brother, **who** serves in the PLA.

## 五、定语从句知识体系速查图

```
定语从句
├── 关系代词
│   ├── who (指人/主宾)
│   ├── whom (指人/宾)
│   ├── whose (指人or物/定)
│   ├── which (指物/主宾)
│   ├── that (指人or物/主宾)
│   └── as (指人or物or整句话/主宾表)
├── 关系副词
│   ├── when (= 介词 + which, 时间状语)
│   ├── where (= 介词 + which, 地点状语)
│   └── why (= for which, 原因状语)
├── 两种类型
│   ├── 限制性（无逗号）
│   └── 非限制性（有逗号，不用that）
├── 特殊结构
│   ├── 介词 + which/whom
│   ├── 名词/代词 + of + which/whom
│   ├── such ... as vs such ... that
│   ├── the same ... as vs the same ... that
│   └── one of + 复数名词（主谓一致）
└── 易混辨析
    ├── 定语从句 vs 同位语从句
    ├── 定语从句 vs 强调句
    └── 定语从句 vs 状语从句
```
