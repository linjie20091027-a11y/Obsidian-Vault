# that 的特殊用法

## 一、必须用 that 的情况

### 口诀
```
代高序，疑问词，人物并（并列），
不定（代词）与众（多修饰）用 that。

还有表语和 there be，
看看下面各条例。
```

---

### 1. 先行词被形容词最高级修饰

当先行词被形容词最高级或 first、last 等序数词修饰时，关系代词常用 that。

> This is **the best** film **that** I have ever seen.
> （这是我看过的最好的电影。）

> He is **the tallest** boy **that** I know.
> （他是我认识的最高的男孩。）

### 2. 先行词被序数词修饰

> The **first** thing **that** you should do is to calm down.
> （你应该做的第一件事是冷静下来。）

> He was the **last** person **that** left the classroom.
> （他是最后一个离开教室的人。）

### 3. 先行词是不定代词

当先行词是 **all, much, little, few, everything, anything, nothing, none, something, the one** 等不定代词时。

> **All that** can be done has been done.
> （所有能做的都已经做了。）

> I have **nothing that** is worth reading.
> （我没有什么值得读的东西。）

> Is there **anything that** I can do for you?
> （有什么我能为你做的吗？）

> **Everything that** he said was true.
> （他说的一切都是真的。）

**特殊：something 后可兼用 which**
> There is something **that / which** I want to tell you. ✅

### 4. 先行词被 the only / the very / the same / the right 修饰

> He is **the very** person **that** I am looking for.
> （他正是我要找的人。）

> That's **the only** thing **that** we can do now.
> （那是我们现在唯一能做的事。）

> This is **the same** museum **that** I visited last year.
> （这就是我去年参观的那个博物馆。）

### 5. 先行词被不定代词修饰（all / every / no / some / any / little / much 等）

> I have read **all the** books **that** you lent me.
> （我读完了你借给我的所有书。）

> There is **no difficulty that** we can't overcome.
> （没有我们克服不了的困难。）

> He has **little** time **that** he can spare.
> （他几乎没什么空余时间。）

### 6. 先行词同时包含人和物

> He talked about the teachers and schools **that** he had visited.
> （他谈到了他拜访过的老师和学校。）

> The writer and his novel **that** you talked about are well-known.
> （你谈到的那位作家和他的小说都很有名。）

### 7. 当主句以 who 或 which 开头的疑问句时

为避免重复，定语从句用 that 引导。

> **Who** is the man **that** is standing at the gate?
> （站在门口的那个人是谁？）

> **Which** is the book **that** you borrowed from the library?
> （哪本是你从图书馆借的书？）

### 8. 关系代词在从句中作表语时

> He is not the man **that** he used to be.
> （他不是过去的他了。）

> My hometown is not the one **that** it was ten years ago.
> （我的家乡不再是十年前的那个样子了。）

### 9. 当先行词在 there be 结构中

> There is a seat in the corner **that** is still free.
> （角落里还有一个空位子。）

### 10. 先行词被 the same 修饰（与 as 的辨析见下文）

> This is **the same** pen **that** I lost yesterday.
> （这就是我昨天丢的那支笔。——同一支）
> This is **the same** pen **as** I lost yesterday.
> （这支笔和我昨天丢的那支一样。——同类，不是同一支）

## 二、不能使用 that 的情况

### 1. 非限制性定语从句中

> ✅ This book, **which** I bought yesterday, is very interesting.
> ❌ This book, **that** I bought yesterday, is very interesting.

### 2. 介词后（介词 + 关系代词）

> ✅ This is the house **in which** I lived.
> ❌ This is the house **in that** I lived.

> ✅ The man **with whom** I went is my friend.
> ❌ The man **with that** I went is my friend.

### 3. 先行词是 all 指人时

> **All who** want to go should sign up. ✅
> **All that** want to go should sign up.（语法正确，但不自然）❌

指人时，先行词用 those 更合适：**Those who** want to go ...

### 4. 在 "those + 定语从句" 指人时

> **Those who** break the law will be punished. ✅
> **Those that** break the law will be punished.（虽然语法上可接受，但更常用 who）🔺

## 三、the same ... as 与 the same ... that

这是高频考点，必须区分清楚：

| 结构 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| the same ... **that** | 同一个（同一事物） | This is the same pen **that** I lost. 这是我丢的那支笔。 |
| the same ... **as** | 同类（不是同一个） | This is the same pen **as** I lost. 这和我丢的笔一样。 |

> He is wearing the same coat **that** he wore yesterday. 
> （他穿着昨天穿的那件外套。——同一件）

> He is wearing the same coat **as** I bought last week. 
> （他穿着一件和我上周买的一样的外套。——同款，非同一件）

---

## 四、such ... as 与 such ... that

这也是极易混淆的考点！

| 结构 | 类型 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|------|
| such ... **as** | 定语从句 | as 在从句中做成分 | such a book **as** you described |
| such ... **that** | 状语从句 | that 不做成分 | such a good book **that** I read it twice |

**对比分析**：

> ① He is **such** a good teacher **as** we all love.
> → as 在从句中作 love 的宾语（love the teacher）
> → 定语从句

> ② He is **such** a good teacher **that** we all love him.
> → that 在从句中不作任何成分（从句 we all love him 完整）
> → 结果状语从句

> ③ This is **such** an interesting book **as** I want to read.
> → as 作 read 的宾语 → 定语从句

> ④ This is **such** an interesting book **that** I want to read it.
> → that 不做成分 → 状语从句

**判断方法**：看从句缺不缺成分
- **缺主语/宾语** → 定语从句，用 **as**
- **不缺成分** → 状语从句，用 **that**

## 五、常见错误辨析

### 错误 1：非限制性从句用 that
> ❌ He failed the exam, **that** made his parents sad.
> ✅ He failed the exam, **which** made his parents sad.

### 错误 2：介词后用 that
> ❌ The house **in that** I lived was old.
> ✅ The house **in which** I lived was old.

### 错误 3：必须用 that 却用了 which
> ❌ This is the best film **which** I have ever seen.
> ✅ This is the best film **that** I have ever seen.

### 错误 4：such ... as 和 such ... that 混淆
> ❌ He is such a man **that** is always ready to help others.（从句缺主语，需要 as）
> ✅ He is such a man **as** is always ready to help others.
> （as 在从句中作主语，定语从句）

### 错误 5：the same ... as 与 the same ... that 混淆
> ❌ This is the same watch **as** I lost.（如果想说"就是丢的那块"）
> ✅ This is the same watch **that** I lost.（同一块表）
